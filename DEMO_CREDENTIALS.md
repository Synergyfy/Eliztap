## Quick Access

1. **Login Page:** `/login`
   - Click "Business Owner" or "Admin" button to auto-fill credentials
   - Or manually enter the credentials above

2. **Business Dashboard:** Automatically redirects after business login
3. **Admin Dashboard:** Automatically redirects after admin login
